Embedding the Feedback Widget

The HouseLearning Feedback Widget lets you collect quick thoughts, bug reports, and improvement ideas from visitors directly on your site. It’s lightweight, fast, and requires zero setup.

What It Looks Like

When you embed the widget, the feedback window will open randomly, providing you with the same window HouseLearning & SafeLibrary uses.

How to Embed the Widget

Drop this script anywhere in your head or right before the body ends: 

<script src="https://houselearning.github.io/feedback.js"></script>

No initialization. No configuration. The widget activates automatically. You can use it on **any website**, including static HTML, GitHub Pages, and frameworks.

Required Credit

To legally use the HouseLearning Feedback Widget on your site, you must include attribution.

Place the credit in your footer:

<p>Feedback system provided by <a href="https://houselearning.github.io/" target="_blank">HouseLearning</a>.</p>

That’s it — one line. As long as the credit is visible on your site, you’re fully cleared to use, modify, and embed the widget.
