SafeLibrary – What It Is

SafeLibrary is HouseLearning’s safe, curated digital resource hub — built for learners, creators, and educators. It bundles together audio, books, interactive programs, images, and kid-friendly AI tools — all under a safe, moderated environment. Everything is hand-picked or filtered to avoid inappropriate content and keep learning spaces clean.

Below you’ll find detailed descriptions of each section, how they work, and how to report anything that slipped through.

Audio Library

A collection of safe, royalty-free sound effects and ambient audio perfect for projects, games, or relaxation. All audio has been reviewed to ensure no inappropriate or copyrighted content — safe for classroom and public use.

Example uses:

  • Background music for games or interactive apps
  • Sound effects for buttons, alerts, transitions
  • Ambient loops for study or relaxation

Books / Audiobooks / Videos

A curated library of free literature, audiobooks, and educational video content. Everything is safe, age-appropriate, and checked for copyright or licensing issues. Great for self-learning, reading practice, or multimedia study sessions.

  • Classic public-domain books
  • Educational audiobooks and read-alongs
  • Instructional or documentary videos (math, science, history, etc.)

Interactive Programs

Browser-based interactive tools like learning games, quizzes, simulations — all built or approved by HouseLearning. They’re sandboxed, safe, and optimized for both fun and educational value.

Use them for:

  • Math / science practice games
  • Learning modules with quizzes
  • Interactive tutorials (coding, logic, creativity)

Safe Images Library

A gallery of hundreds of images cleared for free use — school projects, slides, backgrounds, game development, and more. All images are vetted to ensure no explicit or inappropriate content.

Typical uses:

  • Illustrations for lessons or games
  • Background assets for web pages/apps
  • Visual aids for educational content

Reporting Unsafe Content

If you find something that violates our content standards (explicit material, copyright issues, malware, etc.), please let us know. SafeLibrary relies on community vigilance + automatic filters to stay clean.
