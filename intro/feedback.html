User Interface

The HouseLearning Feedback Window

The **Feedback Window** is the little pop-up you see at the bottom-right of HouseLearning pages. It's our lightweight, zero-cost way to gather suggestions, bug reports, and user ratings — all without needing a backend.

Why It Exists

HouseLearning uses things like Google Analytics and Firebase to run. We want to know how we are doing as a site & as a team. That means we needed a feedback system that:

  • loads instantly on every page
  • stores responses locally until submitted
  • runs on pure JavaScript without a backend
  • feels familiar — like Google's “Send Feedback” popout

How It Works

When you click the **feedback button**, the script opens a smooth, animated panel where you can:

  • rate your experience
  • send a message or suggestion
  • report issues or broken pages

Where Your Feedback Goes

Feedback is sent to a GitHub endpoint used by HouseLearning maintainers. No personal data. No tracking. Just your message — that’s it.
